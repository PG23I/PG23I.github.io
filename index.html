About Me

I'm a Junior in Computer Engineering at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. Over the past summer, I interned at the National Center for Supercomputing Applications where I was responsible for designing a solution to deploy a DNN on a Raspberry Pi to detect human falls. I've also interned at Health Care Engineering Systems Center where I developed a VR Authoring Tool followed by a stint at AirV labs where I designed a cloud-based solution to manage users and devices. I've previously worked in a research lab where I developed tools to process LIDAR and satellite data. Apart from this I'm also a course Assistant for ECE 220: Intro to Computer Systems and Programming. I have a strong interest in Systems and Backend Software Dev and I'm currently looking for Spring/Summer 2021 Internships. Outside of work I enjoy trekking and listening to music.
